Our story

In September of 2007, Flo met Gary outside of her class through a mutual friend. Gary aka 'rageguy' was an introverted third year Computer Science student. He was your typical gadget-loving computer geek and an original Apple-fanboy (before iPhones existed). Flo was a first year Psychology student, excited about reaching her legal drinking age and making new friends. She loved fashion and was an OG-foodie, eating out and posting foodporn before hashtags and smart phones even existed.

After many late night study sessions together, Gary asked Flo out on a date. On their first date, Gary took Flo out cruising (another one of Gary's hobbies) to Ambleside Park in West Vancouver. They would order McDonalds drive-thru and hang out for hours, even watching the sunrise together.

Flo found Gary's blunt, rational and methodological attitude genuine and reliable and his sense of humour quirky. If it wasn't for Gary, Flo would never have started her career in IT where they worked as students together at the Arts Help Desk. Gary thought Flo was pretty and had an appetite 10 times bigger than her size. If it wasn't for Flo, Gary would never have put on a few healthy pounds and tasted as much delicious food as he has now (they both do not enjoy cooking).

As the years went by, they shared their love for food, electronic music and travel together. Having been to Ultra Miami, Ultra Croatia and Paradiso at the Gorge, they have a list of music festivals around the world they want to experience after the wedding, starting with Tomorrowland in Belgium.

The Proposal

In March 2016, Flo went on a girls trip to her favourite city in the world: New York. Little did she know that Gary had been planning the surprise of a life time. On one foggy night, the haggard, tired and flu-stricken Flo was unwilling to go to Top of the Rock with her friends. After much convincing (apparently, there was a lunar eclipse that could only be seen at the top), Flo went up 70 floors. She did not regret it but wished she wasn't in an oversized hoodie with no make up on. Because of the weather, there was no one else but the two of them and they had their New York moment surrounded by a few friends. They left the Top of the Rock engaged and Flo had a rock of her own.

FAQ

Is there parking around Brockhouse?

Yes, there is a large paid parking lot that costs $6.00 a day.


What alcoholic beverages will be served?

Wine will be served with dinner and there will be drink tickets. A cash bar is also available.


What is the dress code?

Semi-Formal to Formal Attire. We recommend a cocktail dress or floor length dress for the ladies and a suit and tie for the gents.


Where will the ceremony be held?

If it's dry, the ceremony will be held outside on the lawn over looking Jericho beach. If it is raining, the ceremony will be held inside the conservatory.


Is there accommodation close by?

The Sheraton Wall Centre is located right off the Burrard Bridge in Downtown and is an 18 minute drive away from Brockhouse. If you want to stay outside of downtown, the Holiday Inn Vancouver Centre is located on Broadway and is a 16 minute drive from Brockhouse.
